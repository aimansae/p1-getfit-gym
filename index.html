    Crossfit

    CrossFit is a training program, introduced by Greg Glassmann the United States in the 1970s. CrossFit is a form of high-intensity training which mixes three different fitness disciplines:

    • weight lifting with barbells, dumbbells, but also with kettlebells
    • free body gymnastics or with tools such as artistic gymnastics rings and the barre
    • aerobic activity such as running, rowing, cycling, jumping rope, but also swimming if possible.
